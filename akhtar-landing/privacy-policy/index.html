3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Monitor usage and diagnose technical issues
  • Communicate with you about service updates, support, and account matters
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and protect against misuse, fraud, or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

Akhtar uses the information you provide, including birth details, conversation context, and prior interactions, to generate personalized outputs. These outputs are produced through software systems and AI models and are intended for reflective, informational, and entertainment-oriented use.

Payments are processed by third-party payment providers. We do not store your full payment card details on our own servers unless explicitly stated otherwise through a secure payment provider integration.

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only as necessary with:

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers

  • Security and fraud-prevention providers

These parties are given access only to the extent needed to perform services on our behalf.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • provide the service

  • preserve conversation history and personalization
  • meet legal, accounting, security, and operational obligations
  • resolve disputes and enforce agreements

We may delete or anonymize data when it is no longer needed.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials and for any activity under your account.
