How to Delete Your Akhtar Account

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the Akhtar app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Account"
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion

What gets deleted:

  • Your account and profile information
  • Your birth chart and astrological data
  • Your saved people and compatibility reports
  • Your chat and reading history
  • All personal preferences and settings

What is retained:

  • Anonymized, non-identifiable usage data may be retained for up to 30 days for fraud prevention and legal compliance purposes.

If you have trouble deleting your account in the app, contact us at: support@akhtar.today

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
